Information Systems Development and Implementation Capstone Project

Here, I am excited to share the details of my Computer Information Systems senior capstone project. Working with a talented team of ten individuals, including back-end and front-end developers, database developers, UX/UI designers, and a system analyst, we created an innovative office hours management system for James Madison University.

Our project aimed to streamline the scheduling and management of office hours between professors and students. We developed a user-friendly platform that allowed professors to upload their office hours, while students could easily sign up for available slots. The system placed students in a queue, providing them with their queue number and estimated waiting time.

To enhance the user experience, we incorporated features such as the ability for students to view their queue number, cancel appointments, and perform other tasks for flexibility. Professors had access to analytics that enabled them to monitor the number of students in the queue and gain insights into their availability.

As the front-end/database developer, my role involved ensuring the website was fully functional and user-friendly. I utilized HTML, CSS, and SQL to create an intuitive interface, collaborating closely with the UX/UI designers to deliver a visually appealing website that met the needs of our users. This project allowed me to enhance my technical skills in front-end development and database management. It also reinforced my ability to work effectively in a team, emphasizing the importance of clear communication and efficient collaboration.

This capstone project provided me with real-world experience in developing a practical solution to a common problem faced by both professors and students. It allowed me to combine my technical knowledge with creativity and problem-solving abilities, resulting in a tangible and impactful product. The project not only deepened my understanding of computer information systems but also fostered a passion for creating solutions that optimize efficiency and improve user experiences.
